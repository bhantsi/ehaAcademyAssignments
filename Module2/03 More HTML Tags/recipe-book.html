Gurasa



Gurasa
Ingredients Details Proceedure
1 and half cup flour Gurasa is a flat bread, made from flour, yeast, baking powder and egg. It can be made with all purpose flour, whole wheat flour or a combination of the two. It is similar to making bread, however, the dought for Gurasa is lighter than that of bread. Gurasa can either be fried or baked in oven using locally made oval earthenware pot know as tanderu . Get a bowl of flour, a pinch of salt, egg, yeast, baking powder, butter, lukewarm water (warm water) and stir well for about 15 minutes. for 1hr 30minutes. When he got up, I twisted it and put the pan on the fire
Half teaspoon of yeast Put the bread on and closed the lid. After a few seconds I opened it and turned it over. There is something in the bottom of the pot. After you have rolled it up, put the paste on the pot and cover it with a lid. Kill it until the pot is hot. Put it on. Get an onion and slice it, slice the green onion and red or whatever you have. Kill the hater
Lukewarm water Start adding the beef stock bit by bit and mix the dan wake ingredients at the same time till you get a soft sticky dough.If you beef stock finishes and you still have not achieved a soft sticky dough,continue with warm water.
Half teaspoon of baking powder Spread the breadcrumbs on top of your bread with your onions and bell peppers. Do not leave bread unattended and pour over food flakes as this will soften it. Others steam after steaming for a few minutes and then lower. Others take the bread and soak it in hot water to remove it, which helps keep it soft. Whatever you do. Eggs and butter will also soften the bread crumbs but if you don't have it, be careful.
1 medium size Onion
1 seasoning cube
1/2 tin of Vegetable Oil
