Welcome to Venice

The full of marble places build on lagoon

In the northwest of Italy lies the small city of Venice. Renowned for it’s beautiful canals and historic buildings, the city is a popular spot on the tourist trail. Anyone who has visited the city will have noticed the lack of any roads, everything here is managed by the waterways, from public transport to emergency services, it’s all done by boat.

List of Beautiful Places to Visit

  • Grand Canal
  • Rialto Brigde
  • Teatro La Fenice
  • Basilica Di San Marco
  • Jewish Museum
  • Lido Di Venezia

List of Amazing Things to See

  1. Boats & Caneos (of different types and shapes)
  2. Brigdes(there are over 200 of them
  3. Beautiful Islands
  4. Cruise Ships
